Summary of Best Shapes for Custom Buttons:

RoundRectangle2D: Great for rounded corner buttons.

Ellipse2D: Perfect for circular or oval buttons.

Polygon: Best for custom polygonal shapes (e.g., triangle, hexagon).

GeneralPath: Most flexible for creating complex or custom shapes.

Rectangle2D: Simple and traditional for basic rectangular buttons.
